================================================================================
MEDTRUST HEALTH — CLIENT VIDEO DEMO SCRIPT
Business-focused | Simple English | Approx. 6–8 minutes
================================================================================

OPENING (0:00 – 0:45)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN: Landing page with AI assistant]

VOICEOVER:
"Healthcare today is busy—for patients and for doctors.

Patients often wait on phone lines, repeat the same symptoms again and again, and struggle to know when they should see a doctor.

Doctors spend valuable time on basic questions before they can focus on real care.

MedTrust Health brings everything together in one simple place—an AI health assistant for patients, a smart patient portal, and a doctor portal that keeps the whole team aligned.

In the next few minutes, you will see how this platform solves everyday problems, saves time, improves the patient experience, and helps doctors work smarter."


SECTION 1 — WHAT PROBLEM IT SOLVES (0:45 – 1:30)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN: Patient typing symptoms in chat]

VOICEOVER:
"Let us start with the problem.

When someone feels unwell, they usually do not know what to do first. Should they rest at home? Should they book a doctor? Which doctor is right for their symptoms?

Often they search online, call the clinic, or wait until things get worse.

MedTrust Health solves this by giving patients a guided conversation—not a confusing form, but a natural chat that understands what they are feeling and guides them step by step.

No long forms. No guessing. Just clear, helpful support from the first message."


SECTION 2 — PATIENT JOURNEY: SYMPTOM CHECK (1:30 – 3:00)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN: Chat conversation — fever and headache flow]

VOICEOVER:
"Watch how a patient begins.

The patient says: 'I have fever and headache.'

The assistant understands and asks simple follow-up questions—how long the symptoms have lasted, how severe they feel, and whether anything else is bothering them.

The patient answers naturally: one to three days, moderate severity, and no other symptoms.

Based on this, the assistant gives practical self-care advice—rest, fluids, and when to seek medical help if things get worse.

It also reminds the patient that this is general health information, not a diagnosis—and to call emergency services if they feel seriously unwell.

This is the first big win: patients get instant, calm guidance without waiting for a callback or sitting in a queue."


SECTION 3 — HOW IT SAVES TIME (3:00 – 3:45)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN: Book appointment flow in chat]

VOICEOVER:
"Now the patient is still not feeling well and wants to see a doctor.

Instead of calling the clinic, searching for availability, and going back and forth, they simply say: 'Book appointment.'

The assistant suggests the right type of doctor based on their symptoms—in this case, a General Physician—and shows available doctors and time slots.

The patient selects Dr. Rajesh Sharma for today at 2:00 PM.

The assistant asks for a quick confirmation, then only needs an email to verify identity.

A secure one-time code is sent. The patient enters it—and they are signed in and ready to complete the booking.

What used to take multiple phone calls and manual coordination now happens in minutes, inside one conversation.

That is how MedTrust Health saves time—for patients and for clinic staff."


SECTION 4 — HOW IT IMPROVES PATIENT EXPERIENCE (3:45 – 5:15)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN: Patient Portal — booking confirmed card, consultation summary panel]

VOICEOVER:
"After booking, the patient is taken to their Patient Portal—a personal space where everything is clear and in one place.

They see a booking confirmed card with their appointment details. From here they can set a reminder, reschedule, or cancel if plans change. No need to call the front desk.

On the right side, they see their full consultation summary: symptoms detected, risk level, clinical recommendations, suggested follow-up, and how long they reported feeling unwell. Everything is transparent and easy to follow.

The patient also receives a confirmation email. The doctor receives one too—so both sides are informed without any extra effort.

The experience feels modern, personal, and reassuring. Patients always know what is happening and what to do next."


SECTION 5 — HOW IT HELPS DOCTORS (5:15 – 6:45)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN: Doctor Portal — dashboard, today's appointments, priority queue]

VOICEOVER:
"Now let us switch to the doctor's view.

On the Doctor Portal dashboard, Dr. Rajesh Sharma sees today's appointments, a priority queue, and a full schedule—all organised for the day ahead.

No digging through files or asking nurses for updates. Everything is visible at a glance.

In the patient list, each patient comes with a short summary—so the doctor knows who is coming and why, before the visit even starts.

With one click, the doctor opens full patient details.

Here they see the complete picture: patient profile, consultation progress, detected symptoms, risk assessment, clinical recommendations, suggested follow-up, reported duration, and the full AI chat history.

The doctor walks into the consultation already informed. They do not need to repeat basic questions. They can focus on listening, examining, and treating.

That means better use of clinic time, less admin burden, and a more meaningful conversation with every patient."


CLOSING — BUSINESS VALUE SUMMARY (6:45 – 7:30)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN: Split view — patient chat, patient portal, doctor portal]

VOICEOVER:
"Let us bring it all together.

MedTrust Health solves a real problem: patients need fast, clear guidance, and doctors need better information before every visit.

It saves time by turning long phone calls and manual booking into a smooth, guided digital journey.

It improves patient experience with instant support, easy booking, email confirmations, reminders, and a portal that keeps patients informed and in control.

And it helps doctors with organised schedules, patient summaries, and full consultation history—so they can deliver better care with less friction.

MedTrust Health is not just a chatbot. It is a complete healthcare companion—for patients who need help, and for doctors who want to give their best.

In our next release, video consultation will be added—for both patients and doctors—so follow-ups and urgent cases can be handled without delay, even when an in-person visit is not possible right away.

Mobile notifications are also on the roadmap, so important updates can reach patients wherever they are.

Thank you for watching. We would be happy to walk you through a live demo tailored to your clinic."

TIPS FOR RECORDING
================================================================================

- Use real demo data (Dr. Rajesh Sharma, sample patient email) for authenticity.
- Keep cursor movement smooth; avoid rushing through OTP and portal screens.
- Background music: soft, professional, low volume under voiceover.
- End with clinic logo and contact / demo request line.
