# MediAI – Intelligent Digital Care Platform

## Overview

An AI-powered healthcare platform that enables patients to assess symptoms, find the right doctor, book appointments, upload medical reports, and receive personalized healthcare guidance through a single intelligent digital experience. The platform combines AI-driven triage, real-time scheduling, patient management, clinical workflows, emergency routing, and telehealth-ready consultation tools to streamline healthcare delivery for patients, doctors, and healthcare organizations — locally and worldwide.

---

## Key Features

- AI-powered symptom assessment and triage
- Interactive conversational healthcare assistant (guest + logged-in)
- Real-time doctor discovery and appointment booking
- Intelligent specialist recommendations based on symptoms
- Natural-language slot selection (e.g. doctor name, date, and time)
- Patient portal for appointments, records, and consultation history
- Doctor portal with schedule, priority queue, and patient insights
- Full AI chat history and pre-visit summary for doctors
- Medical report upload and AI-powered analysis
- Symptom photo upload and visual AI assessment
- Appointment reminders, reschedule, and cancellation
- Automated email notifications for patients and doctors
- Secure authentication with OTP email verification
- Multi-role access for Patients, Doctors, and Administrators
- Admin panel for users, appointments, analytics, and platform control
- Emergency symptom detection with urgent care guidance
- Emergency consultation routing for critical cases
- Mental health crisis detection and urgent therapist / counselor support
- Video consultation (patient and doctor portals)
- Voice-based AI health chat and hands-free symptom intake
- Prescription refill request workflow
- Health education and wellness Q&A
- Consultation progress tracking — symptoms, risk, recommendations, follow-up
- Medical history, allergies, and medication management
- Mobile-responsive web experience
- Multi-language support readiness
